About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional with active CompTIA Security+, CySA+, Network+ certifications and the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. My background includes foundational knowledge in security operations, vulnerability management, and cloud security concepts.

I'm currently building hands-on projects to apply what I've learned:

  • SIEM deployment and security monitoring
  • Vulnerability assessment with Nessus
  • AWS IAM security best practices

My professional focus is on DoD-relevant security frameworks including the Risk Management Framework (RMF) and STIG compliance. I'm working toward these through a structured learning plan that builds on my current certifications and hands-on projects.

I'm also actively exploring how AI tools can augment security workflows—using LLMs to accelerate analysis, summarize findings, and assist with documentation, while learning to identify where AI output needs human verification.

I hold an expired AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification and am currently studying for the AWS Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA-C03) certification as my next step.

The name Imago Dei reflects my belief that humans are created with purpose, creativity, and responsibility. I approach technology with that same mindset: to build with integrity, to solve problems thoughtfully, and to contribute to a safer, more resilient digital landscape.

Personal Identity

My faith shapes how I work, learn, and interact with the world. It grounds me in humility, perseverance, and a commitment to doing what is right—even when no one is watching. Technology is powerful, and I believe it should be used in ways that honor human dignity and promote the good.
