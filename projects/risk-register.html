Risk Register + Heat Map

Overview

A complete risk register for a mock logistics/transport company, built using NIST 800-30 methodology. The register identifies 15 operational, technical, and compliance risks, scores them by likelihood and impact, and documents treatment plans.

Purpose: Demonstrate ability to identify risks, translate technical findings into business impact, and communicate risk to executive audiences.

Artifacts Included

  • Risk Register Spreadsheet: 15 risks with likelihood/impact scores (1-5 scale)
  • Risk Treatment Plans: Each risk assigned a treatment (accept, mitigate, transfer, avoid) with specific action items
  • Heat Map: Visual representation showing risk distribution by likelihood and impact
  • Executive Summary: Top 3 risks with recommended actions for leadership
  • Risk Methodology Document: Scoring criteria and treatment rationale

Risk Example

Risk: Unpatched vulnerabilities in logistics tracking application

Likelihood: 4 (High)
Impact: 5 (Severe)
Inherent Risk: 20 (High)
Treatment: Mitigate
Action: Implement monthly patching cycle. Deploy Nessus scans weekly. Remediate critical findings within 7 days.
Residual Risk: 8 (Medium)

Framework

NIST 800-30: Risk assessment methodology, risk scoring, treatment options
