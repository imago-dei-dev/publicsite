CUI Scoping Criteria

Assets are designated as CUI-relevant if they:

  • Store, process, or transmit CUI (shipment manifests, customer PII, contract data)
  • Provide security controls for CUI-handling assets
  • Are part of the authorized boundary for CUI systems

Assets excluded from CUI scope: Employee personal devices not used for work, guest Wi-Fi networks, systems with no access to CUI.

Protection Status Legend

Encryption

Logging

CMMC Asset Management Alignment

This artifact addresses CMMC Level 2 requirements in the Asset Management domain:

  • AM.1.001: Identify and inventory information system assets (20 assets inventoried)
  • AM.2.002: Ensure that only authorized users have access to assets (CUI designation tracks which assets require controls)
  • AM.2.003: Track and protect assets that process, store, or transmit CUI (CUI status column + protection status)
